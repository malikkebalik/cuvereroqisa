Tom Hardy is an English actor, writer, producer, and model who is best known for portraying roles like Venom in Venom and Venom: Let There Be Carnage, Max Rockatansky in Mad Max: Fury Road, and Alfie Solomons in Peaky Blinders. He has also appeared in various other films and TV shows such as CBeebies Bedtime Stories, Star Wars: The Last Jedi, The Drop, The Dark Knight Rises, Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy, The Inheritance, and This Means War. For his acting skills, Tom has won awards like the British Independent Film Award, Central Ohio Film Critics Association Award, Critics’ Choice Movie Awards, and British Academy Film Awards.
