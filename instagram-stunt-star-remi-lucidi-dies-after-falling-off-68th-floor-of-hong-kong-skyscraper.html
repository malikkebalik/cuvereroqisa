Instagram stuntman Remi Lucidi dies after falling from a building

French stuntman Remi Lucidi tragically died last week after losing a grip on the 68th floor of a Hong Kong skyscraper.

The 30-year-old daredevil who goes by the same Remi Enigma on Instagram slipped outside a penthouse apartment on Thursday evening at the 721-foot-tall Tregunter Tower, as per reports from Sky News and the Independent.

He reached the high-rise and told the security guard he was there to see a friend who lived on the 40th floor.

Sky News then reported that he made his way to the top floor.

The news outlet said that the surveillance camera captured Remi Lucidi exiting the elevator on the 49th floor and again on the staircase to the top floor where he had to force the door open.

Remi Lucidi then somehow got trapped outside the building. He then spotted someone and knocked on the window. She then informed the police about the situation.

But it was too late as the extreme sports enthusiast, who documented his feats with amazing pictures on social media lost his footing and fell to the ground.

The outer revealed that his body and sports camera were found lying on a patio.

A source told The South China Morning Post,

“It is possible that he got trapped outside the penthouse while practicing an extreme sport in the building, and he knocked on the window for help, but accidentally fell to his death,”
