Where Is Darrell Waltrip Now In 2022 And Why Did He Retire?

Darrell Waltrip is enjoying his retirement life in 2022.

It’s been nearly 3 years since his retirement and during these times he remains connected with the fans and responds to a letter he received by sharing his experience and educating about his history.

Now, he rarely attends the racing ceremony, unlike in the old days. Covid-19’s arrival has somehow hampered his real-time affection as well but he believes that he hung up at the right time.
