Larsa Pippen is an American reality star who is identified as the cast of the Bravo reality series The Real Housewives of Miami. Besides, Pippen is widely famous as the ex-wife of former NBA player Scottie Pippen.

The adorable Larsa was born as Larsa Younan to a Syrian-Lebanese family on July 6, 1974, in Chicago, Illinois, USA. She has a younger brother named Sammy Younan. Larsa earned her Bachelor's degree in political science from the University of Illinois.

Celebrity Ex-Husband: Scottie Pippen

The primary reason behind Larsa's prominence is because of her ex-hubby Scottie Pippen. Her eminence rises to fame right after she shared her intimate life with the sports personality, Scottie.

The 6-feet 8-inches tall Scottie is a former basketball player who played for the National Basketball Association (NBA) teams such as Chicago Bulls, Houston Rockets, Portland Trail Blazers, and others.

The NBA Championships winner and the Olympic Gold Medalist basketball player Scottie currently serves as NBA Analyst for ESPN.

About their Married Life

The reality star Larsa and the former NBA player Scottie first started their romantic relationship back in 1995. Nearly after three years of long dating, they tied the wedding knot on July 20, 1997, in Chicago at the First United Methodist Temple.

From the wedding day, Mr. and Mrs. Pippen were sharing a good bond as a perfect married pair. Following the marriage, they shared four adorable kids. 

The husband-wife duo welcomed their first kid, a son, Scotty Pippen Jr in 2000. They shared their second and third kid Preston Pippen and Justin Pippen in 2002 and 2005 respectively. At last, they became parents for the fourth time in 2008 and shared a daughter named Sophia Pippen. 

The Pippen family of six was living the happiest life. Everything was going fine; however, in 2016, Scottie filed for divorce, accusing Larsa of cheating on him with the rapper Future. But Larsa denied the accusation.

Later, the pair dismissed the file. They somehow managed their marital life. However, again after two years in 2018, Larsa filed for divorce. But as per Sources, the duo officially finalized their divorce in December of 2021.

Before sharing an intimate life with Larsa, Scottie was in a marital relationship with Karen McCollum from 1988 to 1990. Karen and Scottie also shared a son named Antron Pippen (b. 1987). Antron passed away in 2021.

Similarly, Scottie shared a son named Sierra Pippen (b. 1995) with his former fiancee Yvette De Leon. Likewise, he has a daughter named Taylor Pippen (b. 1994) born via his relationship with Sonya Roby. Scottie's daughter Tyler with Sonya died after nine days of birth.
