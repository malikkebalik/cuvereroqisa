    Mimi Keene Facts

  • She grew up in England.
  • She made her first TV show appearance as Brandy May Lou in an episode titled Dederama of the children’s comedy-drama series, Sadie J, in February 2013.
  • Between 2013 and 2015, Mimi Keene played the role of Cindy Williams in the soap opera titled EastEnders. For this role, she was nominated for the British Soap Award for “Best Young Performance” in 2014 as well as for the Inside Soap Award for “Best Young Actor” in 2015.
  • She did voiceover work for the video game Castlevania: Lords of Shadow 2 in 2014.
  • Mimi Keene made her theatrical film debut as Claire in the 2019 action thriller film, Close, which starred Noomi Rapace, Sophie Nélisse, Indira Varma, and Eoin Macken.
  • In 2019, she appeared in the main role of Ruby Matthews, one of the school’s popular but mean girls, in the comedy-drama web television series, S*x Education, in which she alongside the likes of Asa Butterfield, Gillian Anderson, Anne-Marie Duff, Emma Mackey, Tanya Reynolds, Kedar Williams-Stirling, and Aimee Lou Wood.
