Who is Rhona Mitra?

Rhona Mitra is an actress, model, singer as well as a songwriter. Her real name is Rhona Natasha Mitra and belongs to British nationality. From 1997 to 1998, she became famous for the Lara Croft model.

Furthermore, she had even known for her roles as Holly Marie of Party of Five in 1999–2000; as Tara Wilson on The Practice in 2003–2004 and the first and second seasons of Boston Legal in 2004–2005; as Detective Kit McGraw on the third season of Nip/Tuck in 2005; Doomsday as Major Eden Sinclair in 2008; Underworld: Rise of the Lycans in 2009; and Strike Back in 2012–2013.
