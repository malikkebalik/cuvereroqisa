This song, “Instagram Live Freestyle” by Playboi Carti, was a freestyle he performed on Instagram Live on his private account in 2018. Although it was not released as an official track, it gained popularity among his fans and showcases Carti’s unique style of rap.

The lyrics of this freestyle emphasize Carti’s confidence and status in the rap industry. The first verse starts with Carti asserting that no one wants to start any trouble with him. He mentions pulling up with a large group of people and having a chopper ring, implying that he has both a strong support system and is not afraid to defend himself. The chorus focuses on Carti’s love for expensive and flashy things, such as shiny jewelry and Chanel bags filled with cash. He also mentions his quick rise to success.

“Instagram Live Freestyle” by Playboi Carti can be seen as a reflection of his confidence and dominance in the rap game. The repetition of certain phrases and the assertive tone throughout the song serve to establish his presence and command respect. It showcases his prowess as a rapper, conveying a sense of power and authority.

Personally, this song reminds me of the importance of self-assurance and staying true to oneself. Carti’s unapologetic confidence in his abilities and success is inspiring. It serves as a reminder to always believe in oneself and not let others undermine our ambitions. The lyrics also reinforce the idea of surrounding oneself with supportive people, as Carti often mentions his group of friends who have his back.

The instrumental and delivery of the song add to its overall impact. The catchy beat and Carti’s unique flow contribute to the addictive nature of the track. It’s a song that has the power to make you feel invincible and ready to take on the world.

In conclusion, “Instagram Live Freestyle” by Playboi Carti is a testament to his confidence, power, and dominance in the rap industry. The lyrics and delivery highlight his self-assuredness and gives listeners a glimpse into his mindset. Whether or not it was intended as an official release, this freestyle has resonated with fans and continues to be an influential part of Carti’s discography.
