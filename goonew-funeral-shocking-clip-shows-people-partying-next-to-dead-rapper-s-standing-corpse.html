Goonew funeral: Shocking clip shows people partying next to dead rapper's 'standing' CORPSE

 Posted on September 7, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1107 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Shocking footage of a bizarre funeral has been doing rounds on the Internet, showing the body of Goonew propped up for display while people partied. The rapper was killed in March in Maryland and while his death shook his fans, the latest clips from his apparent funeral 'party' at Bliss nightclub in Washington DC on Sunday, April 3, has left his fans “disgusted”.

Viral videos and images show what appears to be the remains of the DMV rapper in a standing position at the event. However, there was no official confirmation about whether the body was actually his mummified corpse or a mannequin. The figure that was seen standing had one hand in his pocket and was dressed in ripped pants, a dark blue Amiri sweater with sneakers and had accessories like a watch and a crown.
