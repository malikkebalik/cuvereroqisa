Enes Batur's Career 

Golden Palm Awards winner (2018), Enes Batur, started his YouTube channel on November 18, 2012, named Enes Batur, with 15.3 million subscribers. As a vlogger, he creates videos on him playing games like Minecraft and making challenge videos with his close ones, TikTok videos, etc. 

Furthermore, on November 10, 2013, he has created another vlogging channel under the username Enes Batur 2, which has 1.13 million subscribers. He has only posted eight videos on that channel and has earned over 19 million views.

Youtube star Batur started his acting career by debuting Enes Batur Hayal mi Gerçek mi, released on January 19, 2018. He also played in Enes Batur: Gerçek Kahraman (2019). He made his single "Sen Yerinde Dur (feat. Kaya Giray)" in 2018 and his own movie soundtrack "Dolunay" in 2019.
