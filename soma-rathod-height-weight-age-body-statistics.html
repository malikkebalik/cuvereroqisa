Soma Rathod is an Indian actress, comedian, and YouTube personality who is best known for having been cast as Ramkali Tiwari (Amma Ji), Tiwari’s strict mother who constantly beats him for every mistake and loves her daughter-in-law Angoori, in the &TV Hindi-language sitcom, Bhabiji Ghar Par Hain!. In addition to that, she has also appeared in films and shows such as May I Come in Madam?, Pyar Mein Thoda Twist, Jijaji Chhat Par Hai, and Lapataganj.
