The first season of The Real Housewives All-Stars, which is officially called The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip, did take us to Turk’s and Caicos with Teresa Giudice, and Luann de Lesseps, and Cynthia Bailey. So, who will we see in The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip: Ex-Wives Club, the second season? What we know is as follows.

Ultimate Girls Trip 2 Is It Renewed?

Current Show Status Of the Ultimate Girls Trip 2 Has been Renewed for June 2022.

Ultimate Girls Trip 2 Release Date

Season 2 of Peacock’s The Real Housewives: Ultimate Girls Trip is set to release on June 23, 2022. On June 23, Peacock will launch The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip Ex-Wives Club. 3 of the seven episodes will be released on the day of the debut, with the latest episode available for streaming every Thursday after that.

Peacock will reunite eight of the franchise’s biggest previous performers for a second season of its critically praised all-star spinoff next month, and PEOPLE has an exclusive look at the teaser and cast photographs.

Ultimate Girls Trip 2 Cast

The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip Ex-Wives Club will star Tamra Judge and Vicki Gunvalson from The Real Housewives of Orange County; Dorinda Medley and Jill Zarin from The Real Housewives of New York City; Phaedra Parks and Eva Marcille from The Real Housewives of Atlanta; and Taylor Armstrong and Brandi Glanville from The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.

Ultimate Girls Trip 2 Expected Plot

In the first season of “The Real Housewives Ultimate Females Trip,” several of the usual cast members were up to their normal antics, including Ramona Singer from “RHONY” clashing with, then apologizing to, girls from other large cities. The holiday drama, however, appears to have followed them home as well.

“It doesn’t fly when you keep saying the same thing time after time.” Isn’t that what insanity looks like? Those ‘RHUGT’ ladies had to return home. With Ramona, I’ll be back in New York. We’re both cast members on the same show. She’s one of my cast members. They won’t have to deal with her when they get home.” Luann de Lesseps told Page Six about it.

Ignoring the fact that Season 2 features a whole new ensemble, early media teasers suggest that the cast will be just as turbulent, if not more so. “I told my boyfriend, ‘You know, I could do this again.’ I could do it for the rest of my life; it’s a lot of fun.’ “However, by day four, I’m crying and angry, and I need to leave.” Jill Zarin told WFLA in November 2021. It will take place in Dorinda Medley’s Massachusetts manor, in addition to the tropical beach resort.
