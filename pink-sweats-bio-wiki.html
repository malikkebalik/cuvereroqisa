Who are Pink Sweats?

Pink Sweats is an American singer and songwriter best known for his song, ‘Honesty’ which accumulated over 2 million streams within 8 weeks.

In November 2018, he released his debut EP, ‘Volume 1’.

Pink Sweats: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Pink Sweats was born David Bowden on February 14, 1992, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, the U.S.

As of 2022, his age is 30. There is no information about the identity of his parents and siblings.

He was born to a Christian church-going family. He spent most of his time in the church and grew up listening to church music.

But according to him, he was a little ‘rebellious’.

For him, the choices were either church music or nothing, and his choice was ‘nothing’.

He did not listen to the real music of his choice until he was 17 when he started listening to his icons such as Michael Jackson and Prince.

Later, Kanye West came to the scene and he started listening to his songs because they were not gangsta rap.

He grew up in the hood and did not like gangster rap from his early days.

His ethnicity is African-American.

Education

Sweats is a high school graduate.

Health

After graduating from high school, he suffered from the rare disease Achalasia for three years.

This is the type of disorder that prevents food and other liquids from passing from the esophagus.

It could have also cost his life, fortunately, he was strong enough to battle this disease and start his career.

Pink Sweats: Professional Life, Career

Pink Sweats first started making music when he was 19 as a demo vocalist at Sound Stigma Studios.

He then started writing songs for other artists such as rapper Tierra Whack and country duo Florida Georgia Line.

Initially, he never wanted to become a singer himself.

He co-wrote materials for various artists including Natalie Imani, Rosie Bondz, Aaron Camper (including the 2016 single “Guns and Roses”), and Tierra Whack (“Child Please” and 2018’s “Pretty Ugly”).

But after surviving a three-year battle with a rare esophageal disorder with a near-death experience, he felt inspired to start singing and signed a digital distribution deal with Human Resources.

He made his record debut in July 2018 with ‘Honesty’ and ‘No Replacing You’.

‘Honesty’ gained attention on streaming platforms and reached #10 on Spotify’s U.S. Viral Chart and accumulated over 2 million streams within 8 weeks.

He released ‘No Replacing You’ on July 26.

His stage name, Pink Sweats was adopted as he always wore pink sweatpants while recording.

Talking about the origin of his famous name, he recalled,

I would come to the studio for a couple of months, and I was on this pink wave where I would wear these pink sweatpants every single day.

This dude, didn’t know my name, and I wasn’t around, and he was like, “Yo, where’s pink sweats?” And then someone told me and I was like, “Wait, that’s fire. If I was ever an artist, that would be my name.”

On November 2, 2018, he released his debut EP, ‘Volume 1’. On March 28, 2019, he released its sequel EP, ‘Volume 2’.

On May 25, 2022, Pink performed at the 14th Seoul Jazz Festival.
