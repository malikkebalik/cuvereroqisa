Who is Courtney Hadwin?

Courtney Hadwin is a British Singer. She became an overnight sensation after her show-stopping performance on ‘America’s Got Talent.

Additionally, she received the Golden Buzzer from Howie Mandel.

Courtney Hadwin’s Early Life, Childhood

Courtney Hadwin was born in Hartlepool, England on July 6, 2005. She is the daughter of Paul and Annmarie Hadwin.

In addition, she became interested in the world of music in her childhood years. She is of British nationality. Furthermore, she belongs to an English ethnic background.

In which school did Courtney Hadwin attend?

Talking about her education, Courtney Hadwin attended the Academy at Shotton Hall, Peterlee, County Durham, England.
