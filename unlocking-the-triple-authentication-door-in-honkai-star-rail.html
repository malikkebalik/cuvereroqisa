Unlocking the Triple Authentication Door in Honkai Star Rail

 Posted on September 15, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  788 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The Challenge of Honkai Star Rail Triple Authentication

Are you ready to tackle the space station challenge in Honkai: Star Rail? One of the game’s objectives involves opening a securely locked door in the Base Zone area of the Herta Space Station. To access this door, you’ll need a triple authentication verification card, which can be obtained by combining three separate access authentication cards.

Your journey to unlock the triple authentication door begins in the Base Zone section of the Herta Space Station. Upon arriving at the door, interact with the controller positioned in front of it. You’ll receive instructions to complete the triple authentication verification card before gaining entry.

To secure the first authentication card, seek out an NPC sporting an afro within the Master Control Zone area of the space station. Engage in conversation with him five times, and he’ll present you with the initial card.

For the second authentication card, venture to the Storage Zone area and locate a console to unlock a door behind you. Inside, you’ll face a light bridge puzzle that must be solved to progress. At the puzzle’s conclusion, you’ll find the second card awaiting you.

The final authentication card can be earned by completing the “Road to Revival” side quest within the Master Control Zone area. Progress through the quest until reaching the objective of “activating the extinguished anchor in the target cabin”. Upon completion, return to the Base Zone area and confront a formidable enemy to acquire the last authentication card.

Upon obtaining all three authentication cards, a notification will indicate your success, and you’ll receive a message from the Herta Panopticon System. Make your way back to the triple authentication door, interact with the controller, and witness the door swing open. Inside, battle and defeat the two Voidrangers to lay claim to the chest containing valuable rewards.

Accessing the Triple Authentication Door in Honkai Star Rail

Upon collecting all three access authentication cards, a notification from the Herta Panopticon System will signal your accomplishment. Return to the triple authentication door and interact with the controller to unveil what awaits beyond.

As you step inside, prepare to face two voidrangers, whose defeat is necessary to progress. Subdue them, and access the chest within to claim its rewards, aiding you in your future endeavors.

It’s essential to be well-prepared before obtaining the access cards and taking on the voidrangers. Equip yourself with the proper weapons and abilities, and ensure you grasp the game’s mechanics to triumph in this challenge.

Solving the Mystery of the Triple Authentication Door in Honkai Star Rail

Delve into the locked chamber of the Triple Authentication Door concealed within the Herta Space Station by acquiring three Authentication Cards. Learn where to locate these crucial cards below.

Authentication Card #1 Discovery

Embark on a search for the initial access card in the Master Control Zone of the Herta Space Station alongside the man with the Afro near Wen Shiqi. Engage in five conversations with him to secure the card.

Authentication Card #2 Expedition

To locate the second access card, journey to the Storage Zone within the Herta Space Station and initiate the unlocking process at the Outside the Control Center (First Floor) waypoint. Solve the light bridge puzzle by activating the control devices to access the second card in a newly opened room.

Authentication Card #3 Pursuit

Seek the final access card within the Master Control Zone of the Herta Space Station. Progress through the Road to Revival side quest until completing the “activate the extinguished anchor in the target cabin” objective. Return to the Base Zone, vanquish a potent foe, and claim the last access card.

Unveiling the Triple Authentication Door

After accumulating all three Authentication Cards, venture back to the Triple Authentication Door in the Base Zone of the Herta Space Station. Engage with the controller, opening the door for exploration.

Encountering the Voidrangers

Inside the chamber, confront and conquer the two voidrangers awaiting you to gain access to the chest. Ensure your readiness with appropriate tools and skills before engaging them.

Reward of Success

Having triumphed over the voidrangers, seize the chest’s contents, offering treasures and power-ups to bolster your adventures.

The Allure of Honkai Star Rail Triple Authentication Room

Unlock the Triple Authentication Room in the Base Zone of the Herta Space Station within Honkai: Star Rail by assembling the essential triple authentication verification card. The room, housing valuable rewards, is a coveted destination for players seeking loot in their gameplay journey.
