Who Is Bold &amp; Beautiful's Annika Noelle (Hope) Dating?

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  326 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Love is a fickle thing. That’s something The Bold and the Beautiful‘s Hope knows after loving a fickle man (Liam) and a troubled one (Thomas)! But hey, burned over and over again or not, at least Hope is giving love a chance with Thomas’ marriage proposal. We guess all that Emma stuff is water under the bridge. Whether or not she really wants to spend the rest of her life with Thomas, though, well, we’ll just have to wait and see how enduring they are!

Luckily, we’re happy to say that it seems Annika Noelle is faring a whole lot better when it comes to love in real life. In fact, the actress even took to Instagram to share her beau with the world just in time for Valentine’s Day! Now that’s how you do a reveal.

If a picture’s worth a thousand words, then get ready for a whole lot of talking about love as Noelle shared not just one, but two photo booth strips with actor John Patrick Amedori cute enough to melt the stoniest of hearts! Check the happy couple out below.

If you’re thinking Amedori is a bit familiar, then you’re probably right, as the actor’s been in a slew of movies and television shows, from TV fare like The Good Doctor and Gossip Girl, to films like Scott Pilgrim vs. the World and The Butterfly Effect.

Noelle, clearly feeling the day and the love, went on to share romantic clips on her Instagram stories from Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, It’s a Wonderful Life and the King himself, Elvis Presley, singing “I Got Lucky” in Kid Galahad. In other words, it’s one happy day for Noelle, and a big win for love!

We couldn’t be more thrilled for the happy couple and wish them the best Valentine’s Day ever!
