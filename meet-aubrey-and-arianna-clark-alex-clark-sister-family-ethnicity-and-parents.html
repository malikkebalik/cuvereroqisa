Alex Clark Sisters Aubrey and Arianna came to the highlight all because of their brother. In his cartoon, My Evil sister Aubrey is shown as an evil sister who appears to ruin Alex’s perfect days.

Alex Clark, generally known as Its Alex Clark, is an American YouTuber who predominantly uploads animations.

The self-taught animator is also a comedian, former street performer, and animator. He and his team are responsible for creating his animations and running his self-titled YouTube account.

In 2014, he started a YouTube channel called AlexClarkLive, where he films vlogs and performs live streams, during which he sketches out upcoming videos.

In addition to winning four People’s Choice Awards, Clark is a nominee for the 2017 Streamy Awards.

Likewise, his writing has been praised as “Brilliant” by HuffPost and “funny” by the Chicago Tribune.

Alex Clark Sister: Aubrey And Arianna Clark

Alex has two sisters named Aubrey And Arianna Clark. His younger sister, Aubrey, inspires Alex’s villainous character sister in his cartoon. 

Her character frequently messes up his ideal day, as demonstrated in his cartoon videos.

After watching his cartoon series, people have asked Alex about what his sister is like in real life. In his animated video, which was posted in 2018, he claimed that his sister is not evil and way more sweet in real life. 

Even though Aubrey may have poked him a little and pranked him during their childhood days, they get along just fine as adults. Moreover, she has never harmed him in any way. 

Fun fact: As she is portrayed as the evil sister in Alex’s animations and cartoons, this made a few of her assistants fearful of her.

Apart from this, the YouTuber hasn’t talked much about his other sister, Arianna Clark. She seems like a private person avoiding attention as much as possible. 

Alex Clark Parents, Family Ethnicity And Childhood

Alex Clark is from a Caucasian family. Sadly, his parents’ identities remain unknown at the moment.

The comedian seems like a secretive guy, which explains why he hasn’t said anything about his parents or other family members.

He attended the high school in Massachusetts for the performing arts with his sister. The sibling duo jokes about one another all the time.

Moreover, Clark had entered a contest in middle school to “draw the mayor’s Christmas card” when he was a kid and won it by creating a card he saw at Hallmark.

Despite all the practical jokes they had played on one another, he started working with his sister as a teacher at the same school.

The Youtuber and Animator learned how to juggle while still in school and mentioned that he aspires to excel as an animator and lead a Disney production in the future.
