Kai Cenat Facts

  • He had joined the gaming-focused YouTube group AMP along with fellow YouTubers JustFanum, ImDavisss, Duke Dennis, Agent 00, and Chrisnxtdoor.
  • In the past, Kai started his clothing line, Mafia Worldwide Clothing.
  • The earliest post that Kai uploaded to his Instagram account was in January 2017.
  • He joined the YouTube community with his self-titled channel on December 26, 2012. However, the first post that he uploaded to his channel was an introduction video on January 14, 2018.
  • In February 2021, he began streaming on Twitch.
  • On February 1, 2021, Kai started his secondary YouTube channel titled KaiCenat Live.
  • As of March 2022, some of the most-watched videos on Kai’s YouTube channel were I Became A Professional Cuddler & Made $700, I Told Teanna Trump To Come To The Backseat, Fake Producer Prank On Famous Rappers!, Teanna Came To My Room, and How To Die In Every Hood!.
  • In May 2022, he released a new song, Bustdown Rollie Avalanche featuring NLE Choppa.
  • In June 2022, he was featured in Polo G‘s single Distraction.
  • He has featured several celebrity guests in his streams like Bobby Shmurda, Lil Baby, and 21 Savage.
  • In December 2022, he won the “Streamer of the Year” award at the 12th Streamy Awards.
  • In February 2023, he became the most-subscribed Twitch streamer of all time, with more than 306,621 subscribers.
  • In April 2023, he was temporarily suspended on Twitch for undisclosed reasons. However, he eventually returned to the platform.
  • During the 2023 Streamy Awards, Kai was honored with the “Streamer of the Year” award.
