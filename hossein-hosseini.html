Biography

Hossein Hosseini was born in Shiraz, Iran on June 30, 1992. Iranian professional football player who is known for his work as a goalkeeper on the Esteghlal club as well as Bargh Shiraz. He is also known for his work as a keeper on the Iran national team as well. Other goalkeepers also known for their work on the Iran national team include Alireza Biranvand and Rahman Ahmadi. He started his youth career playing for the Bargh Shiraz club in 2006. He made his senior career debut with the club in 2010. In 2012, he started playing with the Esteghlal club as well as the Iran under 23 national team. He moved up to the official Iran national team in 2018.
